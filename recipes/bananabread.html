Ingredients
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt

How to make:
  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. Combine bananas, sugar, egg, and butter together in a bowl.
  4. Mix flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl
  5. Stir into banana mixture until batter is just mixed. Stir in salt
  6. Ppour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  7. Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 60 minutes, or until soft and bubbly.
  8. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
