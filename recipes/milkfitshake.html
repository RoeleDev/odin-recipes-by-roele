Greetings, this fit shake is very good to lose weight, and feel well feed

Not a lot of people know about this recipe, because its simple

But if you try to make one for your self you might fall in love with it, because how fast and easy you can make it

Ingredients
  • milk 500 ml
  • bananas 1 or 2
  • curd 200 grams

How to make:
  1. Grab a fresh milk, with fat not more then 1%, 1 cup(something around 500 mL)
  2. Grab curd and combine it with milk
  3. Cut two or one bananas in half
  4. Mix two above and curd in a any suitable place, where you can blender them afterwise
  5. Blender it untill everything is liquid, and then you can drink from there or another step for style points
  6. pour the fitshake in nice glass and put there fancy straw. Well done +10 style points
